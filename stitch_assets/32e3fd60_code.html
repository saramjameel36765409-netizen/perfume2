Our Heritage

A Legacy Carved in Scent

For over a century, Luxe Aura has redefined the olfactory landscape. Rooted in the rich soils of Grasse and the bustling ateliers of Paris, our journey began with a singular obsession: to bottle the ephemeral. We do not merely mix oils; we compose symphonies of emotion, relying on generational knowledge passed down through the hands of master artisans.

The Art of Extraction

True luxury demands patience. We employ ancient enfleurage techniques alongside modern molecular distillation to capture the absolute purest expression of each botanical. Every drop in a Luxe Aura flacon represents thousands of petals, harvested by hand at the precise hour of dawn to ensure peak olfactory resonance. It is a laborious, uncompromising process that yields unparalleled depth.

The Ingredients

Sourced globally. Selected obsessively. The foundation of every masterpiece.

Oud Assafi

Sustainably harvested from centuries-old Aquilaria trees. Deep, smoky, and infinitely complex.

Sambac Jasmine

Plucked at dawn in Tamil Nadu. Intoxicatingly sweet with a subtle, animalic undertone.

Calabrian Bergamot

Cold-pressed rind. The sparkling, bitter-green opening note that demands attention.

Baltic Amber

A fossilized resin. Imparts a profound, enveloping warmth and sensual longevity.
